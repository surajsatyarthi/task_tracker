## ✨ App Highlights

### Eisenhower Matrix
Organize tasks into 4 quadrants:
- **Do First** (Red) - Urgent & Important
- **Schedule** (Green) - Not Urgent & Important
- **Delegate** (Orange) - Urgent & Not Important
- **Eliminate** (Blue) - Not Urgent & Not Important

### Smart Features
- ⚡ Drag & drop between quadrants
- 🔍 Real-time search
- 📱 Mobile optimized
- 🎨 Color-coded projects
- ⏰ Overdue task alerts
- 💾 Auto-save to database
- 🔐 Secure authentication
