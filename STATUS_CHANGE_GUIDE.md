## How to Change Task Status

### Method 1: Click on the Task (Recommended)
1. **Click on any task** in the Matrix/Table/Calendar view
2. **Task Details Modal** will open
3. Look for **"Quick Actions"** section at the bottom
4. Click on any status button to change it instantly

### Available Status Options:

| Status | Icon | Label | Color | Use Case |
|--------|------|-------|-------|----------|
| `todo` | 📝 | To Do | Gray | Tasks not started yet |
| `doing` | 🔄 | In Progress | Blue | Currently working on |
| `done` | ✅ | Done | Green | Completed tasks |
| `on_hold` | ⏸️ | On Hold | Yellow | Paused/waiting |
| `help_me` | �� | Help Needed | Purple | Blocked/need assistance |

### Method 2: Edit Mode
1. Click on a task to open details
2. Click **"Edit"** button (top right)
3. Change any field including status
4. Click **"Save"** when done
